FAQs

When and where do I pay monthly dues?
By the 15th of each month via Zelle: (801) 719-4750
How many privates a week do I need?
1 mandatory private and 1 mandatory semi-private per week. Pitchers and Catchers may do additional work.
How do I sign up for privates?
Use the Lessons tab on this site. If a slot is full, contact that player directly to arrange a swap — do not delete other players.
Can parents attend practices?
Privates and Semi-Privates are closed to parent viewing. For Fielding at T&C, sit in right or left field — not behind the dugout.
Can parents engage during games?
No coaching from the stands. No approaching the dugout. General cheering is always welcome! Players must be self-sufficient during games.
When does my player need to arrive?
At least 30 minutes early. Players must be warmed up and ready when coaches arrive at start time.
Is play time guaranteed?
No. Play time is earned during practice. There is a 24-hour post-tournament contact rule — please wait before reaching out about anything from that weekend.
Can my daughter play for other teams?
No.
What shoes at tournaments?
Turf shoes when not in cleats. No crocs.
